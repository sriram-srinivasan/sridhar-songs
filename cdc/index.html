Club Dances and Cabarets

Sridhar Krishnamoorthy is, among other things, a theoretical physicist, fiction author, educator and, an incredible database of Hindi films, particularly of the 'golden era' (40's - 70's).

On his facebook page, Sridhar shares wildly entertaining rambles of movie trivia along a number of different axes. I (sriram srinivasan) wanted to mentally track all the names and the ways in which they are intertwined, so I started mapping them visually as 'relationship graphs'.

This page contains all the maps of his series on Club Dances and Cabaret songs. It is meant to accompany Sridhar's writeup on FB.
